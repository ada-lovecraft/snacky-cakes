Introducing Two Rooms and a Boom

Easy To Learn

Your first game of 2R1B will only take 6 minutes, and in no time you’ll lying, conniving, spying, and hustling to your heart’s content.

Fast To Play

Games are a great icebreaker, but people can be afraid to commit a large chunk of time to a game they’ve never played before. Fortunately, every game of 2R1B plays in fifteen minutes or less.

Any Group: Big or Small

Corporate event? Birthday party? Church group? Regular gaming night? Even if you’re throwing a party where the attendees don’t know each other, 2R1B plays with 6-30+ players, and gets people up and talking!
