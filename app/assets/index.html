Introducing Two Rooms and a Boom

Two Rooms and a Boom is a social deduction, hidden role game. There are two teams, the Red Team and the Blue Team. The Blue Team has a President. The Red Team has a Bomber. Players are equally distributed between two rooms. The game consists of 5 timed rounds. At the end of each round, some players will be traded into opposing rooms. If the Red Team’s Bomber is in the same room as the President at the end of the game, then the Red Team wins. Otherwise the Blue Team wins.

How To Play

What is this game?

Two Rooms and a Boom is a social deduction, hidden role game. There are two teams, the Red Team and the Blue Team. The Blue Team has a President. The Red Team has a Bomber. Players are equally distributed between two rooms (separate playing areas). The game consists of 5 timed rounds. At the end of each round, some players will be traded into opposing rooms. If the Red Team’s Bomber is in the same room as the President at the end of the game, then the Red Team wins. Otherwise the Blue Team wins.

Step 1: Setup the deck.

The character deck includes the Bomber card, the Blue President card, and an equal number of Red and Blue Team cards. There will be one character card in the game for each player in the game.

Step 2: Randomly Give Out the Character Cards

Each player receives one character card face down. The character cards are secret but can be revealed during the game. Players should not discuss their character cards with one another until the game begins.

Step 3: Two Rooms...

Randomly separate the players into two rooms as evenly as you can. The “rooms” can be separated by a wall, door, or simply two open areas at a distance from each other. It is not important if players can see one another, but separate rooms shouldn’t be able to hear one another.

Step 4: Start The Timer

At the end of each round, the room leader selects a number of hostages to change rooms. The game concludes at the end of the 1 minute round after the last hostage exchange.

Step 5: ... and a Boom!

At the end of the last hostage exchange, everyone reveals their character cards. Whichever room has the Bomber in it will explode, killing everyone inside. If the President is in the room with Bomber, then the entire Red Team wins, if not, The President is safe and the entire Blue Team wins.
